Flower Tea
胎菊

Chrysanthemum Bud

The young unopened buds of chrysanthemum flowers have a refreshingly sweet and lingering pleasant aroma. Chrysanthemum Bud has medicinal properties which are known to help enhance vision and complexion.

Origin
Xi Hu, Zhejiang Province

Appearance
Dried light yellow coloured fine unopened chrysanthemum buds. Infusion is light yellow.

Taste
Very refreshing natural slightly sweet, pleasing aroma and taste.
