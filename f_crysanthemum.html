Flower Tea
菊花

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum tea is one of China’s most popular Herbal teas. This tea is well-known for its cooling properties that help to decrease body heat and is recommended for those with fever, sore throat, and other heat-related illnesses.

Origin
Xi Hu, Zhejiang Province

Appearance
Dried light yellow coloured chrysanthemum flowers. Infusion is light yellow.

Taste
Very refreshing natural slightly sweet, pleasing aroma and taste.
