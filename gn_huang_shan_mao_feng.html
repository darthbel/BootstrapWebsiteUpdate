Green Tea
黃山毛峰

HUANG SHAN MAO FENG

Huang Shan (Yellow Mountain) is one of China's famous mountain for producing great green teas and Mao Feng is undoubtedly one of the best. This tea is one of China's ten famous tea.

Origin
Fuding, Fujian Province
 

Appearance
Emerald-green young and tender tea leaves consist of one bud one leaf and one bud two leaves. The infusion is light yellow.

Taste
This tea features a floral aroma, lightly sweet with a mild nuttiness and a clean finish.
