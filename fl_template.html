Flavour Tea
 

LYCHEE RED TEA

Lychee Red Tea is made from tea which has been scented with lychee peels. Lychee is a sweet tropical fruit that is popular in Asia.

Origin
Guangdong Province

Appearance
Small dark brown twisted leaves. Tea infusion has a reddish brown colour.

Taste
Strong pleasantly sweet fruity aroma and honey-like taste.
