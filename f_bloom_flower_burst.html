Black Tea
一六八班章老樹餅茶


Yunnan Menghai 168 Banzhang Old Tree Pu-erh Tea Cake – a limited edition raw green tea cake comes with a traditional elegant gift box. This tea cake is specially blended with spring harvested silver buds for Chinese Tea Culture Canada Inc. This premium green tea cake is for your immediate enjoyment or collect and age.



Origin

Yunnan Province Banzhang Mountain is located in southwestern Xishuangbanna at 1800 m above sea level. This is a natural, wild type tea forest. A variety of wild herbs and plants grow around the base of the tea trees contribute to Banzhang tea’s rich and unique flavour.


Appearance

The infusion is amber in colour, and will turn to a light red as the tea matures over time.


Taste

A smooth and earthy, pleasantly refreshing, lingering light Pu-Erh taste.
