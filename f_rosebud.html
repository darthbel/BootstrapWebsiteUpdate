Flower Tea
玫瑰花

Rose Bud

This Herbal tea is made from real rose buds, plucked when they are young and then dried. The essential oils in Rosebud Tea can help to improve circulation and is good for the heart.

Origin
An Hui Province

Appearance
Small, dried red rose. Infusion is light amber.

Taste
Natural sweet rosy taste and aroma
