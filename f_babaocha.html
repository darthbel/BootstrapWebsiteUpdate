Special Blend - 8 Treasures Tea

Ba Bao Cha with Green Tea, Chrysanthemum, Red Date, Goji Berry, Longan, Raisin, Rose Bud, Osmanthus

This special blend of eight ingredients is a great thirst quencher and it has medicinal properties that help disperse chills and lower fever, reduce cholesterol, aid blood circulation, strengthen the body's immune system and relieve fatigue.

A full-bodied harmonious combination of fruity, floral and smoky aroma gives this tea a very unique and intriguing flavour.
