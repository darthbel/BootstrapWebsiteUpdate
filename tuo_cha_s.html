Black Tea
普洱小沱茶

PU-ERH BOWL-SHAPED TEA "TUO CHA"

Raw/Green Tea leaves are tightly compressed into bowl-shaped and individually wrapped. Xiaguan Tea Factory has a long standing history of over 60 years producing some of the best Pu-Erh. This tea can be kept for aging. Pu-erh tea is revered for its medicinal qualities, aids digestion, lowers cholesterol and controls obesity.

Origin
Yunnan Province

Appearance
Tea leaves pressed into small cakes shaped like bowls or "birds nests", approximately 100g size. The brew is dense and reddish-dark brown.

Taste
Smooth, sweet, refreshing aftertaste.
