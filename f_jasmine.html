Jasmine - Xiang Pian

Jasmine tea is honored as the most fragrant and popular scented Chinese tea in the world. Jasmine tea is made from good quality green tea leaves which is repeatedly scented with fresh jasmine flowers, allowing the tea leaves to absorb the fragrance, producing a tea with lingering taste and aroma.

Origin
Fuding, Fujian Province

Appearance
Tightly rolled tender silver buds and leaves with some jasmine petals. The infusion is light yellow in colour.

Taste
Refreshingly sweet and delicate tea with a distinct fresh Jasmine fragrance and taste
