White Dragon Pearl

 

White Dragon Pearl is made by rolling the tender shoots of the tea leaves and buds of the Big White Trees of Fuding into "pearls" and infused with Jasmine flowers.

Origin
Fuding, Fujian Province

Appearance
The fine downy tea buds with white jasmine stripe are rolled into beautiful pearl-like shape. When steeped, the "pearls" slowly release a light and wonderful floral aroma.

Taste
A soothing tea that is sweet, creamy, smooth and refreshing, with a delicate jasmine flower scent.
