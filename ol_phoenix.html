PHOENIX SUPREME - FENG HUANG DAN CONG

Dan Cong is a very old tea which history record dates back to 900 years ago. The tea plants are believed to be a specimen of the Shui Xian strain that have been carefully selected to breed as Dan Cong's tree, which is a single trunk tree that rises tall and straight up, and with branches that open out like an umbrella. Feng Huang Dan Cong was also an Imperial tribute tea during the Song dynasty. This tea is partially oxidized.

Origin
Anxi, Fujian Province
Appearance
Slightly curled, long golden-brown twisted tea leaves. The infusion is amber in colour.

Taste
The dried tea leaves of Feng Huang Dan Cong are very aromatic even before they are steeped. The infusion yields a brew that is sweet and smooth long lasting finish.
