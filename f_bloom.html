Flower Tea

BLOOMING TEA

Origin

An Hui Province


Appearance

Silver needle green tea leaves are sewn into a tightly packed ball, meticulously hand-crafted to delight all senses. Each bloom measures approximately 3cm by 3cm. Upon infusion, the tea leaves slowly open up like a blooming flower. Blooming tea is best enjoyed in a glass teapot or tall clear wine glass for maximum visual effect.


Taste

Sweet and smooth taste with delicate aroma.
