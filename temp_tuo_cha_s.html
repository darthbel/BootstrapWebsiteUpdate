普洱沱茶


PU-ERH BOWL-SHAPED TEA "TUO CHA"



Raw/Green Tea leaves are tightly compressed into bowl-shaped and individually wrapped. Xiaguan Tea Factory has a long standing history of over 60 years producing some of the best Pu-Erh. This tea can be kept for aging. Pu-erh tea is revered for its medicinal qualities, aids digestion, lowers cholesterol and controls obesity.

 

Origin

Yunnan Province


Appearance

Tea leaves pressed into small cakes shaped like bowls or "birds nests", approximately 100g size. The brew is dense and reddish-dark brown.


Taste

Smooth, sweet, refreshing aftertaste.



Teapot Size 100ml ~ 500ml (3 - 6 Oz)
Tea Quantity 1/3 of teapot, purple clay or porcelain tea pot is preferable 一六八班章老樹餅茶
Water Temperature 100° C
Procedures Step 1: Heat up the serving teapot by adding boiling water
Step 2: Put in the tea leaves
Step 3: Add water until the teapot overflows. Discard this water as to clean and heat the tea leaves
Brewing Time 1st round 5 seconds
2nd round 10 seconds
3rd round 20 seconds
4th round 20 seconds
5th round 30 seconds
6th round 30 seconds
7th round 60 seconds
8th round 60 seconds
